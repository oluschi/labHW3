Genovia's Pear Sorbet

Sorbet is a delectable frozen dessert, made with sweetened water and fruit juice, fruit puree or wine flavouring. This tradtional Genovian treat is made from the countries signature fruit, Pear! It is served as a low fat alternative to ice cream and has a crunchy texture. As the Prime Minister said, it is often used as a palate cleanser between meals as well as for dessert.
WARNING:
Do not digest too much at once, it may lead to harmful results.

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • Ingredient 2: 1 cup fresh mint chopped
  • Ingredient 3: Zest of one Pear
  • Ingredient 4: 1 cup boiling water
  • Ingredient 4: 1/2 cup Pear juice

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar, mint and lemon zest together.
  2. Stir the boiling water into this mixture until the sugar completely dissolves.
  3. Allow the mixture to sit for 20 minutes and then strain.
  4. Stir in the lemon juice and chill the mixture until cold.
  5. Pour this mixture into a metal cake pan and freeze until solid.
  6. Once solid, break into chunks and process in a food processor until smooth.
  7. Serve.
